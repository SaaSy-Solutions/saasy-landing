## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**Not slop — genuinely differentiated.** Copy passes the second-order test decisively; the demo world is *coherent* (Sundial Bakery appears in customer-detail AND the Ask SaaSy tables; the WH-347 screenshot, CSV, and copy describe the same six workers). The evidence chain — screenshot → full-size → downloadable CSV → contact form honoring the promise — was called "a genuine market rarity… marketing → product handoff with zero broken promises." The honesty strip "reads as a founder's confident bet, not a disclaimer." Residual tells: the `gradient-text` class keeps getting flagged (it's been solid pink since the redesign — the class NAME misleads every fresh reviewer), homepage trust bar is claim-shaped.

**Detector (B):** CLI clean; 15 raw findings → one real item (an ~87ch fine-print line, already fixed) + the three known non-issues (alpha-stop FP re-confirmed at 5.7:1, deliberate single font, footer input-in-box heuristic).

## Priority Issues

1. **[P0] The CSV fails its own "check the math" invitation** — Sam Okafor's Gross All Jobs ($2,545.16) < Gross This Project ($2,545.20), logically impossible; run-total off by $0.04. Cause: seeded OT rate truncated where the product rounds. A compliance officer WILL open Excel.
2. **[P1] Debug residue in the flagship artifact** — "· plans" raw fringe_election + unformatted "$12295.48" in the app's run header (product bug; violates "practice what we preach" at the highest-scrutiny pixel).
3. **[P2] /unions annual prices float context-free** — "$79/mo billed annually" with no toggle/anchor; does "$248 all in" survive annual billing ($198)?
4. **[P2] gradient-text class-name confusion** — the effect is neutralized but the name triggers the anti-reference flag on every review; rename it.
5. **[P3] Homepage trust bar generic**; SSN column blank without a "masked in this sample" note.

## Strengths

1. Evidence chain audits clean end-to-end (except the 4 cents): full-size 200s, CSV 200s with correct types, "$4,600/month" is the actual sum of the three critical accounts.
2. Honesty-as-positioning with spine — converts absence-of-logos into founder-access value.
3. Ask SaaSy screenshot finally delivers "answers cite the actual records."
