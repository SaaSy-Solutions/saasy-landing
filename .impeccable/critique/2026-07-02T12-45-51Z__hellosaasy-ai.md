## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**Visual: PASS.** Effect purge verified in CSS (solid accent word, no glow/pulse/glass). **Copy: PASS with residue** — em-dashes gone from prose; sass lands ("Late fees fund other people's software"); one recurring tic left: the "instead of N tools/tabs" construction 3× on /features.

**Deterministic scan (B):** CLI clean. Browser: 14 findings / 4 pages, all three root causes known — low-contrast ×6 **confirmed false positive** (detector reads the 7%-alpha hero-gradient stop as opaque; measured composited contrast 5.69:1, passes AA), single-font ×4 deliberate (Poppins is the brand font), nested-cards ×4 = one shared footer newsletter box (input inside bordered card). B also notes the union-dues inner panel on /pricing is a real card-in-card the detector missed.

**Screenshot integrity: best-in-class.** A audited the reshot media to the dollar: card MRRs sum to exactly the $20,625 Total MRR; the 6 at-risk accounts sum to exactly $10,495 MRR at Risk; avg of the 12 health scores = 50.75 ≈ displayed 51; Sundial ARR $15,480 = 12 × $1,290; renewal date matches the grid countdown. "Earn trust with specifics," executed.

## Priority Issues

1. **[P0] Live videos contradict the reshot posters.** The playing hero/feature clips still show the old empty-state footage ($0 MRR everywhere, "No tenant-wide health scores computed") because video URLs are overwritten in place under `Cache-Control: immutable, max-age=1y` — the Tigris edge keeps serving the old bytes indefinitely. Plus one real content bug: HeroLoop overlays a hardcoded "Avg. health 82" (HeroLoop.tsx:203) over a screenshot that says 51. **Fix:** content-address the video filenames (pattern already exists for the whats-new clip), fix the 82→51 counter, re-render, update videoAssets refs.
2. **[P1] ask-saasy.png is an empty state** ("No past conversations yet", no answer shown) beside copy promising "Answers cite the actual records they came from." Reshoot mid-conversation (blocked partly by #2652 — tables render raw, multi-turn fails).
3. **[P1] Integration claims drift** — FAQ (Jira in rollout) vs /features prose (no Jira) vs /features JSON-LD featureList ("Stripe, HubSpot, and Salesforce integrations" as shipped, Slack dropped). Single source of truth.
4. **[P2] Residual zeros inside churn-alerts.png** — "0/0" users chip on all 12 cards while customer-detail shows "Active users 2"; also $1800.00 vs $1,290 money-format inconsistency (product bugs).
5. **[P2] Default Next 404** — a sassy branded 404 is a free brand moment.
6. **[P3] Hero video pause control** (WCAG 2.2.2); demo email visible in screenshot sidebars.
