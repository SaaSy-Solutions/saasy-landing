# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Owners and operators of small-to-mid businesses (5–200 employees) — construction GCs, unions, professional services, e-commerce operators — evaluating whether SaaSy can run their back office. They arrive from search, referrals, or outbound; they are busy, skeptical of AI hype, and comparing against both point tools (CRM, billing, payroll) and "do nothing." The job to be done: decide in under two minutes whether SaaSy is credible, understand what it actually does, and start a 14-day trial or book a demo.

## Product Purpose

hellosaasy.ai is the marketing site for SaaSy, a unified multi-agent Business OS: AI agents (virtual C-suite) that handle customer health scoring, churn prediction, proactive alerts, CRM, billing, payroll, grants, and vertical workflows. The site exists to convert SMB visitors into trials ($49/$149/$399 monthly tiers, 14-day trial, no free tier) and to make an ambitious AI product feel trustworthy rather than vaporous. Success = trial signups and demo bookings from qualified SMBs.

## Brand Personality

Confident, warm, expert — with a streak of sass. The name is "SaaSy" and the voice should own that: playful confidence, not corporate solemnity and not clownish. An expert advisor who's earned the right to be a little cheeky. Emotional goals: relief ("someone finally has my back office"), trust (real product, real numbers), and momentum.

## Anti-references

- **Generic AI-SaaS template *effects***: gradient-text heroes, glowing bordered cards, pulsing CTAs, grid-pattern overlays, identical icon+heading+text feature grids — the saturated 2024–2026 AI landing default. SaaSy sells to plumbers and union locals, not to VCs. **Nuance**: the dark navy + pink/orange *palette itself is the official brand* (see Brand Constraints below) and is NOT the anti-reference; the banned material is the effect layer and the generic voice laid on top of it.
- **Crypto/web3 hype aesthetics**: vague grand claims, neon glow.
- **Enterprise corporate**: stock-photo handshakes, jargon walls, feature-matrix density. Buyers are owner-operators, not procurement committees.
- **AI-startup vaporware smell**: claims that outrun the product. The platform is real (live billing, payroll, verticals); the site must feel like it.

## Brand Constraints (from SaaSySolutions_StyleGuide_v1-2.pdf, Oct 2023)

- **Palette is fixed and official**: `#A4293F` rose · `#E45074` pink · `#DB6E36` orange · `#FFFFFF` white · `#1A2435` dark navy. Colors outside the palette are off-limits; **tints and shades within it are allowed** (this is the sanctioned route to WCAG-AA-passing CTA fills, e.g. white on `#A4293F` ≈ 7:1).
- **Pink `#E45074` is reserved as an accent for main CTAs** — the guide itself forbids pink-drenching every surface.
- **Typography**: brand font is Sofia Pro (Black = headlines, Medium = sub-headlines, Regular = body), but **the Sofia Pro license is not held — Poppins is the guide's designated fallback and is therefore the deliberate primary font**, not a compromise to fix.
- **Sanctioned accents**: geometric shape combinations and bright, high-contrast photography — not glows, gradients, or glass.

## Design Principles

1. **Earn trust with specifics** — real screenshots, real numbers, real vertical workflows beat abstract AI promises.
2. **Sass in the copy, discipline in the layout** — personality lives in voice and moments of delight, not in visual noise.
3. **Respect the owner-operator's two minutes** — clear hierarchy, one primary action per screen, no scavenger hunts.
4. **Show the agents working** — the differentiator is AI that *does* things; demonstrate outcomes, don't list features.
5. **Practice what we preach** — a platform selling operational excellence must feel fast, polished, and bug-free.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA: ≥4.5:1 text contrast (≥3:1 large text), full keyboard navigability, visible focus states, `prefers-reduced-motion` alternatives for all animation, labeled controls, semantic headings. Audience skews non-technical and mid-age; favor legible type sizes and unambiguous affordances.
